Step 01
Deposit KAVI
Approve & send any KAVI amount. 5% goes as a deposit fee, 5% to your referrer (or fee wallet). The rest is your net staked principal.
Step 02
Wait for Windows
Your first claim window opens after 30 + days-since-deploy days. After that, every 30 days a new window opens for 40 months total.
Step 03
Claim Monthly
Each month pays 1/40th of 2× your net deposit. The contract picks the lower of: fixed KAVI quantity or USD-value-equivalent KAVI at current price.
Payout Calculation

Every claim, the contract picks the lower of two options:


Option A — Quantity: 2 × net / 40 months in KAVI


Option B — Value: (2 × net × deposit_price / current_price) / 40


If KAVI price rises → Option B wins (fewer KAVI, same USD value as at deposit time).


If KAVI price falls → Option A wins (fixed KAVI quantity, lower USD value).
